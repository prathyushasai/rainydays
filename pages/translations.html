Novel: The Rebirth of the Malicious Empress of Military Lineage (重生之将门毒后)

Chapter 86: Massacre (/第八十六章 屠杀) - Extra

English:
Luo Xue Yan: Must not have too much government influence, must not be too wealthy, must not be too ambitious, must not have a complicated position in government, moral conduct must be upright and honest and must not bully you.

Xie Jing Xing: Cannot meet any of my strong mother-in-law’s conditions. _(:3」∠)_

As a result, the bad egg1, Little Hou Ye in the first round suffered a complete elimination. ╮(╯_╰)╭
