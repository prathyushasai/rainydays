About Me

Hi! My name is Eve! I am an avid reader, curious thinker and programmer. Often times, I even have bursts of creativity as well. I currently speak 3 languages including French and English and am learning my 4th - Chinese.

This website is designed to attempt to encompass my avid reading, curiosity and propensity for all things creative, from photography to art to my writing. The novels page contains a current list of my favorite novels (most of which I have painstakingly or obsessively read to completion) and is soon to be filled with my personal reviews. Please note that this website is a work in progress.
