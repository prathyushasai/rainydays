About Me

Hi! My name is Prathyusha Charagondla! I am a second year Computer Science major at UC Berkeley. I have a vested interest in Data Science and Machine Learning and am pursuing this interest both in academia and outside. Check out my projects for more of my pursuits!

Outside of academia, I am involved with the corporate and industrial relations side of Society for Women Engineers and ANova, a Computer Science Education club focused on educated students from underresourced communities. Check out my resume for more details!

My hobbies are dancing an Indian Classical Dance called Kuchipudi, playing the piano and practicing yoga. I also love learning new languages - whether they are foreign languages or programming languages and traveling and exploring new cultures and countries. I am currently learning my 4th language - Chinese! I have so far visited 8 different countries including Singapore, Saudi Arabia and France.
